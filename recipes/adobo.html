Adobo

Description

My favorite dish to make when I have some time and am craving something that reminds me of childhood. Salty, savory, and juicy, this dish comes together easily.

The sauce would also be great as a marinade. Serve this dish with rice and your favorite veggies (I like roasted brocolli.)

Ingredients

  • Boneless skinless chicken breast
  • Chicken Broth
  • Onion, sliced
  • Minced garlic
  • Minced ginger
  • Brown sugar
  • Soy sauce
  • Rice wine vinegar

Steps

  1. Add oil, ginger, and onions to a pan and cook until onions are soft
  2. Add garlic and cook for another minute or two
  3. Add soy sauce, chicken broth, brown sugar, and rice vinegar to pan. Stir to combine.
  4. Nestle the chicken in the sauce and add to a 350 degree oven for 20-30 minutes, or until chicken is cooked through.
  5. Remove from oven and return sauce and chicken back to stove on medium high heat. Allow sauce to bubble and reduce until it becomes a sticky, syrup like consistency. Serve over rice.
