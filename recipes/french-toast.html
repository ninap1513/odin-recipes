French Toast

Description

Ah, french toast. My favorite breakfast food as a child and still one of my favorites to make on a lazy Sunday morning. It's quick, cheap, and delicious. Top with your favorite syrup, butter, fruit or powdered sugar. Yum!

Ingredients

  • Bread of choice
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Cinnamon
  • Butter
  • Toppings of choice

Steps

  1. In a shallow dish, whisk together eggs, milk and cinnamon until well combined.
  2. Dip bread in the mixture, coating both sides.
  3. Melt butter in a pan and add coated bread, cooking for 2-3 minutes per side, or until golden brown.
  4. Top with desired toppings and serve!
