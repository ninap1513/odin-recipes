Pasta Bake

Description

This is one of my favorite recipes to cook my partner and I. It's quick, easy, and delicious.

It may not be the healthiest dish on the planet, but it's good old fashioned comfort food! Serve it with a salad if you're feeling healthy.

Ingredients

  • Rigatoni pasta
  • Marinara sauce of choice
  • Heavy cream
  • Sugar
  • Garlic powder
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Mozarella cheese
  • Breadcrumbs

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 Degrees
  2. Boil pasta according to package instructions
  3. Drain pasta and return to pot on low heat
  4. Add sauce , cream, and seasonings to your liking
  5. Add sauced pasta to baking dish. Top with cheese and breadcrumbs
  6. Bake for 22 minutes or until golden brown
